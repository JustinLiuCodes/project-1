Who Are Pugs?

Pugs are small, affectionate dogs with big personalities. Known for their wrinkled faces and expressive eyes, they are one of the oldest breeds, originating in ancient China. Pugs were bred to be companions for royalty and have carried their charming and loyal nature into modern homes.
